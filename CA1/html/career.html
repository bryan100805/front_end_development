Opportunities Provided by SP

In SP, students in their second and third year can collaborate with external companies through internship opportunities and projects collaborations. My course, DAAA, even has an alternative path in second and third year, INC (INDUSTRY NOW CURRICULUM), in which students who thrive in their studies can choose this path and get to work as software developers at an industry-facing student agency known as Project INC on real client industry projects to gain credits. All these opportunities, along with the knowledge gained from stuyding in SP, will open a lot of doors to many career options.

Brief Description of Careers Choices

DAAA

Data Analyst:
Data Analyst analyzes raw numeric statistics & data and uses it to help companies make informed decisions.

Data Engineer:
Data Engineer are responsible for data preparation, in which they develop, construct, test & maintain data architecture to be analyzed.

Data Scientist:
Data scientist analyzes, prepare and interpret complex data, both structured and unstructured. They are also responsible for assisting companies to make informed decisions.

Machine Learning Engineer:
Machine Learning Engineers are highly skilled programmers who construct AI systems that use huge data sets to research, develop, and generate algorithms that can learn and make predictions.

AI Applications Developer:
AI Applications Developers integrate the functionality of AI into software applications. They are responsible for integrating and executing AI algorithms and logic into the requirements of an IT project.

Business Intelligence Specialist:
Business Intelligence Specialist is responsible for managing data retrieval and analysis within a company. Their duties include organizing data, communicating between upper management and the IT department and analyzing data to identify what the company needs.

DISM

Associate Security Analyst:
Security Analysts monitor, prevent, and stop data breaches. Their roles include constructing and implementing firewalls and software systems to protect data and networks.

Cyber Risk Analyst:
Cyber Risk Analyst is responsible for planning and executing security protocols for networks, servers, and computer systems. They will also implement ways to improve the digital security systems on a timely basis.

Security Operations Analyst:
Security Operations Analyst works with an organisation to identify and reduce security risks to their computer network. They also need to install security measures to prevent breaches, and give the organisation suggestions about preventing future incidents.

Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing Analyst:
Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing Analyst identifies weaknesses in networks and software and find solutions to correct and strengthen security within the system. They also plan out different scenarios and conducts tests in order to view the effeciency of the system security.

Security Engineer:
Security Engineer identify threats and weaknesses in the company's IT systems and software architecture and prepare for potential threats that could delay the company's operations.

System Support Engineer:
System Support Engineer does further analysis of failing devices by simulating failures. They will also come up with operation and maintenance solutions to satisfy customers' requirements.

Software Engineer:
Software Engineer is responsible for analyzing and modifying existing software, as well as designing, constructing and testing end-user applications to fulfil the users' needs through software programming languages.

IT Auditor:
IT Auditor is responsible for analyzing and assessing a company's technological infrastructure to ensure software is running smoothly and accurately, while following their regulations. They also have to spot for any IT issues and report their findings to the management team, as well as offer solutions to resolve the issues.

Incident Investigator:
Incident Investigator conducts site investigations and collect evidence on the scenes where an incident, accident, or crime had occurred.

Forensic Investigator:
Forensic Investigator collects and analyzes evidence related to legal matters and criminal cases. They also have to consolidate reports about their findings and may have to testify in court.

DIT

IT Analyst:
IT Analyst handle the analysis, maintenance, and improvement of information systems. They develop information solutions and give professional advice to the company on IT strategies.

Software Quality Assurance Engineer:
Software Quality Assurance Engineer monitors every phase of the development process to ensure that the design and software adhere to company standards. They also break up the development process into achievable testing goals and report any issues to the management team in order to meet deadlines on time.

DevOps Engineer:
DevOps Engineer works with developers and the IT staff to monitor the code releases, leading to an understanding in both engineering and coding sectors. They must be familiar with the software development lifecycle and have a clear understanding of various automation tools for developing digital pipelines (CI/ CD pipelines).

Embedded Systems Engineer:
Embedded Systems Engineer are responsible for developing and implementing software for embedded systems such as routers and televisions. They are involved in the entire software process, from building the code through, to final testing and device implementation.

Scrum Master:
Scrum Master acts as a communicator between product owners and teams during large projects. They help with scheduling every step of the entire process of the project whenever necessary.

Project Manager:
Project Manager are responsible for planning and monitoring projects to ensure they are completed on time and within budget.

UI Designer:
UI Designer work on the design and function of a website or app to make it aesthetically pleasing to the user, they are also known as front-end web developers.

UX Designer:
UX Designer works on web-based applications to ensure that they are user-friendly and functional. They also construct applications which are simple and enjoyable for consumers to use.
