A little history about me...

In Primary School, I was not really excited about studying but was more interested in sports related stuff. So, I will always play tag and soccer with my classmates and friends during recess and holidays. From Primary 1 to Primary 3, my CCA was Chinese Orchestra. I used to play the ErHu and the Yang Qing. Throughout that duration, I realised that this wasn't for me and it was also quite stressful trying to balance the practice hours and doing school work. Hence, in Primary 3, I changed my CCA to Robotics Club. I loved being able to build robots using the EV3 set and MindStorms Software to program the robots. In Primary 4 & 5, I even joined a few competitions like the Sumo Robot Competition in Science Centre. Even though I did not win any of them, it was still quite enjoyable and meaningful to me as I learnt a lot of things about robots and programming.

In Secondary School, my CCA was Media Productions Team. I joined the Video section of my CCA where I had to film videos during school events and edit them afterwards. I took on a leadership role of being the Vice President of my CCA. It was a lot of work as I had to prepare content for each session, as well as conduct them, while preparing for examinations. Nonetheless, it taught me skills like presentation as well as planning for budget.
